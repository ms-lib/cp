## NOTES FOR DESIGNER

### Color Palette:
- Warm, cheerful colors (coral pink, sky blue, sunny yellow)
- Playful, not too pastel
- High contrast for easy reading by kids

### Illustration Style:
- Cute, cartoon-style superhero theme
- Diverse mom representations
- Superhero elements (capes, badges, stars, shields)
- Kid-friendly imagery

### Font Suggestions:
- Headlines: Bold, playful, rounded sans-serif
- Body: Clear, readable, kid-friendly (14-16pt minimum)
- Handwritten style for personal sections
