- Bright, energetic colors (multi-color palette)

- Playful, trivia-style design
- High contrast for readability

### Illustration Style:
- Fact cards, badges, ribbons
- Number-focused graphics
- Quiz/trivia game aesthetic
- Playful icons and symbols
- Diverse mom illustrations

- Headlines: Bold, fun, attention-grabbing

- Number fonts for statistics
- Variety for different fact categories
