- Photo-friendly backgrounds

- Vintage photo frames and albums
- Watercolor memory vignettes
- Nostalgic floral elements
- Timeline or journey motifs
- Elegant, sentimental design

### Font Suggestions:
- Headlines: Elegant serif with vintage feel

- Handwritten script for personal moments
- Photo caption style fonts
