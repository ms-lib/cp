## NOTES FOR DESIGNER

### Design Philosophy:
- **Tone:** Energetic, cute, and full of wonder.
- **Color Palette:** Bright primary colors (Comic Red, Sky Blue, Sunshine Yellow) but kept soft for school.

### Technical Constraints (Canva Safety):
- **No Effects:** Strictly avoid Shadow, Lift, and Neon text effects for better printing.
- **60% Rule:** Maintain 40% white space within text boxes for better editability.
